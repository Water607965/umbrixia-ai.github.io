Privacy Policy

Your trust matters. Here’s how Umbrixia collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information like your email address and performance data only when you sign up, login, or use our tutoring features.

2. How We Use It

We use this data to personalize your learning experience, provide analytics, and improve platform functionality. We never sell or share your data without your consent.

3. Third-Party Services

Umbrixia may use services like Firebase, Auth0, or Stripe. Each service has its own privacy policy governing how your data is handled.

4. Data Security

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow best practices to keep your information safe from unauthorized access.

5. Cookies

We use cookies for session management, personalization, and analytics. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you're a parent and believe your child provided data, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You can request to view, edit, or delete your data at any time by contacting support@umbrixia.ai.

8. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy as our platform evolves. You’ll always find the latest version here.
